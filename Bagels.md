#recipe #cooking 

Ingredients:

**Dough**
- 297g (1 ⅓ cup) water 
- 10g (1 Tbsp) active dry yeast
- 23g (1.5 Tbsp) granulated sugar
- 500g (3.5 cups) bread flour, plus extra for bench flour
- 6g (1.5 tsp) kosher salt

Water for boiling:
- water
- 1 Tbsp baking soda 
- 1/4 cup sugar

**Topping ('Everything' Bagels):**
- 2 Tbsp garlic flakes
- 2.5 Tbsp onion flakes
- 1.5 Tbsp white sesame seeds
- 1 Tbsp poppy seeds
- 1 Tbsp black sesame seeds
- 1.5 tsp flakey sea salt

Method:
1. Mix warm water, sugar and yeast, rest for 10 mins
2. add to flour and salt, mix until shaggy dough forms
3. Knead for a long time until dough is smooth and silky (minimum 10 minutes)
4. Form into taut ball, oil bowl and lest rest there for 1 hour or until doubled
   
5. Punch down, turn out and divide into 8 equal pieces
6. make each piece into a taut ball, then rest on tray (covered) for 10 mins
	1. Punch a hole in each ball and create doughnut shape

From here either proof overnight, move directly into boiling and cooking them:
1. Boil water with other ingredients
2. drop a batch of bagels, 1-2minutes each side (longer for chewier)
3. pull out, brush with egg and sprinkle with topping
4. Cooking for 20 mins in 220 degree oven or until golden brown
