#cooking #recipe 

**For 350g pasta** (4 serves)

**Sauce Ingredients**
- 120g butter
- 2 shallots
- 2 Tbsp Ssamjang
- 1 Tbsp tomato paste
- ~20 prawns prawns,  finely chopped 
- 3 small garlic clove, minced 
- Lemon zest (around ¼-½ tsp)  
- Lemon juice (around ¼-½ tsp)  
- Chives, finely chopped, reserve some for garnish 
- Parmesan cheese 


**Method**

1. Cook spaghetti or similar pasta, reserve pasta water

2. Brown butter on med until golden but not too dark, take off heat
3. On low add shallots until fragrant and slightly softened
4. Off heat melt in the tomato paste / ssamjang
5. add prawns, lemon zest/juice and garlic on medium heat until prawns opaque

6. transfer pasta into the pan directly, and a little pasta water and parm, toss to emulsify sauce
7. Add chives, taste and adjust seasoning

8. garnish with chives to serve
