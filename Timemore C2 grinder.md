#### Grind Sizes:

NOTE:
- '0' is measured from the **finest** burr size possible. Rotate clockwise until you can't go further, that is '0'
- Adjust this based on the roast level (lighter roast = finer grind = lower clicks)

V60 - 24 clicks
AeroPress - 13 clicks
