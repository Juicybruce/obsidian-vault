Beth, I've known for a very long time that I want to spend the rest of my life with you. It's sometimes shocking to even me how quickly after some random blind date we were set up on I fell head over heels for you. 

Every time I've looked at you, or even thought of you, since the day we first met, I get this vast and fathomless feeling in my chest. An ache, a longing, a need, a bright burning so exquisitely close to pain, but so far from it. That feeling grew so quickly, but even after 7 years together it still grows every day.

I have no idea how I could possibly articulate it here. How could one possibly capture how I feel about our incredible life together without standing here for hours. But how do I love you? I suppose to count the ways:

- I love your eyes, so dazzlingly blue. I know exactly the shade of the blue, how it changes in sunlight, how intense it becomes when you have been crying.
- I love the way those eyes look at me across a dinner table, after we are just finishing the first glass and second snack course. I feel sometimes that's when you love me the most. 
- I love how those eyes sparkle when we are being silly, when we are sharing some stupid  in-joke we have been workshopping far too long, but we both find inexplicably hilarious, because we are both audience and comedian.
- I love your laugh, every time I hear it I fill full to bursting with joy and pride that I can cause such a sound. I love it all the more when I can make you laugh hard enough to snort
- I love that the most mundane task imaginable becomes something I look forward to because it is with you. Grocery shopping? I never tire of it, I can't wait to pretend to have a fight in the pasta aisle every time we walk through it for the rest of our lives.
- I love the way that sometimes when I stroke your hair while you are tucked into my shoulder, you fall asleep and drool all over me, then get embarrassed about it when you wake up; even though to me you you look like a perfect drooling angel
- I love how unbelievably positive you are, so able to rise above stress and difficulties, often dragging me up alongside you with your grace.
- I love how excited you get to talk to me about things you are passionate about, and while that thing is often Taylor Swift, I still find myself deeply invested in the deeply unhinged conspiracy-level analysis of film clips I haven't seen, because I just love listening to you talk.

And with all of these things I love, it isn't just adoration for you I feel; you give to me a deep inspiration.

I get to wake up every day and experience your grit, your determination, your disciple, your focus. You are someone who decides to do something, to better yourself in one of a myriad of ways and then you just work until you achieve it. Whether that is training to run a marathon, or reading hundreds of books (only a small portion of which are fairy smut).

You make me want to reach further, to meet my expectations of myself, because I see you work so hard and I am so deeply proud of you and I want you to be proud of me in the same way. 

It's a gift to be around you because your mere presence lifts me up, strengthens me to be more worthy of it every day. You inspire me to work hard to be the best person I could possibly be,  and now to be the husband you absolutely deserve:
