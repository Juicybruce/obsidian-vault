### TEXT

7 years 
blind date

I have this vast and fathomless feeling in my chest, every time I think of you. I have no idea how I could possibly articulate it here. How could one possibly capture how I feel about our incredible life together without standing here for hours

So I'm not sure how to capture it, except maybe to count the ways I love you.

- I love your eyes, so piercingly blue. And I know exactly the shade of the blue, how it changes in sunlight, how intense it becomes when you have been crying.
- I love the way those eyes look at me across a dinner table, after we are just finishing the first glass and second snack course
- I love how those eyes sparkle with glee when we are being silly, when we are sharing some stupid joke we have been workshopping far too long, but we both find inexplicably hilarious, because we are both audience and comedian.
- I love your laugh, every time I hear it I fill full to bursting with pride that I can cause such a sound. I particularly love when I can make you laugh hard enough to snort
- I love that the most mundane task imaginable becomes something I look forward to because it is with you. Grocery shopping? Amazing, can't wait to pretend to have a fight in the pasta aisle. Daily commute? Incredible, you always have something interesting in that head of yours we can talk about until we are **forced** to stop by getting to work
...

And with all of these things I love, it isn't just adoration for you I feel. I feel inspired by you.

Every day I get to wake up to the gift that is life by your side, and it spurs me on to be a better, stronger person. 

Your grit, your determination, your focus, your discipline. I get to see it all, every day and it drives me. It makes me want to reach further, to meet my expectations of myself, because I see you work so hard and I am so deeply proud of you and I want you to be proud of me in the same way. 

I adore being around you because your mere presence lifts me up, inspires me to be more worthy of it every day.

I guess that is kind of the crux of my vow to you. I wont waste the inspiration I feel when I look at you, I'll work hard to be the best husband I could possibly be, to be a husband you absolutely deserve:

WHAT I PROMISE

FINAL MELODRAMATIC FLOURISH

I think usually, vows like these promise that these feelings will endure until the day that I die. 
But for what I feel when I look at you, this love that fills me to bursting and  makes the universe feel small.....
that length of time is inadequate.

Bethany, 
I love you to the depth and breadth my soul can reach 
and I will love you until the very last star burns out.
